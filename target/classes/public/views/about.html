About RFM

RFM Stands for Restaurant File Maintenance (RFM). It is a web based application that is used to make data changes to the POS System (NP6). RFM populates Point of Sale (POS) registers in restaurants with product changes, promotions, and other relevant information from a central location, in a secure and reliable way. It's mainly divided in three parts.

  1. Global RFM
  2. US RFM
  3. APMEA RFM. (More than 738 markets working under).
APMEA stands for (Asia Pacific, the Middle East and Africa).
Japan is the country with the largest number of McDonald's restaurants in Asia Pacific, the Middle East and Africa with almost 2,911 units.
The RFM application consists of:
  • A web-based application for data management
  • An integration component to manage store bound data delivery and store originated data delivery
  • An in-store capability to manage local data delivery and support local restaurant data editing Together, all components make up the complete RFM application.

About RFM2 Team Management Portal

A Restaurant File Maintenance (RFM) Team management system (or RFMTMS) is software that helps to keep a record of all members who are part RFM application. It track member details, and keep a record of technologies they are good with.
